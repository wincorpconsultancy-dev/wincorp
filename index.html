About Us

Our Partnership: WINCORP brings together two industry veterans with complementary expertise. V. Shanmugam, founder and proprietor of WINCORP, and S. Jayachandran, founder of Shri Raagatex (SRT), have joined forces to deliver world-class consulting and solutions to the textile industry.

Their combined experience of over 80 years in textile manufacturing, finishing, engineering, and business consultancy enables WINCORP to offer comprehensive expertise across the entire textile value chain.

Our mission is simple yet powerful: build efficient, competitive, and future-ready production environments that help our clients succeed in the global textile market.

Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our consulting services

What industries do you specialize in?

We specialize in knitwear/hosiery, woven textiles, dyeing & finishing, garment production, printing, and technical textiles. Our expertise covers the complete textile manufacturing ecosystem.

Can you assist first-time entrepreneurs?

Absolutely! We provide end-to-end support including business planning, machinery recommendations, vendor network development, workforce training, and ongoing operational guidance. We've helped numerous first-time entrepreneurs successfully launch their textile businesses.

Do you provide on-site support?

Yes, we provide comprehensive on-site support throughout your project. We coordinate directly with your team, suppliers, and technicians during implementation to ensure seamless execution and successful outcomes.

Can you improve productivity in existing factories?

Yes! We specialize in operational optimization for existing facilities. Our services include bottleneck diagnosis, workflow redesign, waste reduction strategies, and throughput improvement initiatives that deliver measurable results.

What geographies do you serve?

We primarily serve Coimbatore, Tiruppur, Erode, and surrounding textile clusters in Tamil Nadu. We also provide extended consulting support across India for clients in other regions.

Do you help with machinery selection and vendor connections?

Absolutely! We leverage our extensive network of trusted suppliers and industry connections to help you procure reliable equipment and establish strong vendor relationships. Our vendor-neutral approach ensures you get the best value and service support for your investment.
