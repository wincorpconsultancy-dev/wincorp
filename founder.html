Our Partnership

A powerful alliance of complementary expertise.

WINCORP brings together two industry veterans with complementary expertise. V. Shanmugam, founder and proprietor of WINCORP, and S. Jayachandran, founder of Shri Raagatex (SRT), have joined forces to deliver world-class consulting and solutions to the textile industry. Their combined experience of over 80 years in textile manufacturing, finishing, engineering, and business consultancy enables WINCORP to offer comprehensive expertise across the entire textile value chain.

V. Shanmugam

Founder Wincorp

40+ years of experience Engineering & Manufacturing Expert Business Consultant

Founder's Profile

V. Shanmugam brings extensive experience across engineering, textile manufacturing, and business consultancy spanning over four decades.

  • 1978-1992: Head of Engineering at Vijaya Foundry, Coimbatore, manufacturing Pumps & Motors. Successfully expanded marketing operations in Andhra Pradesh.
  • 1993-2009: Operated a Knitting Mill in Tirupur on Commission Work for Exports.
  • 2009-2018: Administrative Head of Shri Raaga Tex (SRT), consultants for the Knitting & Finishing Industry in India. SRT were dealers for Finishing & Laboratory Equipment from world leaders in Germany, Switzerland, Italy, and South Korea, setting up more than 40 world-class projects both in India and abroad with know-how transfers and training.
  • 2018-Present: Promoted WINCORP as Sole-Proprietor, providing consulting services to IT services companies and serving as a mentor to business groups in the Textile Industry, developing sales strategies and expansion plans that drive revenue growth and market share.
